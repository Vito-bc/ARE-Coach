Privacy Policy

ARE Coach · Effective June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the ARE Coach mobile application ("the App") collects, how it is used, who it is shared with, and the choices you have. The App is provided by Vitaliy Tei ("we", "us", "our"). ARE Coach helps architects prepare for the NCARB ARE 5.0 exam.

1. Information we collect

Account information. If you sign in with email and password, we collect your email address. If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive an Apple-provided identifier and, if you allow it, your name and an email address (which may be Apple's private relay address). You may also use the App anonymously, in which case we generate a random identifier and do not collect your email or name.

Profile information. Your display name and target exam date, if you choose to provide them.

Study activity. Your quiz and mock-exam attempts (questions seen, answers selected, scores, time spent), per-division accuracy analytics, flashcard progress, and study-streak data.

AI Coach content. When you use the AI Coach, the text you type is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a response. Your prompts and the generated answers are stored in your account so your chat history persists. If you use voice input, your device's speech-recognition service (provided by Apple or Google) converts your speech to text, which is then used as your Coach prompt.

Purchases. If you buy a subscription, the purchase is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive the purchase and subscription status and a receipt, which we validate on our server to unlock premium features. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Diagnostics and security. We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports (stack traces, device model, OS version, app version) to fix bugs, and Firebase App Check (a device-integrity token) to confirm requests come from a genuine instance of the App. We also keep operational usage counts (daily/per-minute request counts and AI token counts) to enforce free-tier limits and prevent abuse; message content is not included in those logs.

Stored only on your device. App settings, flashcard progress, and your study-reminder time are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us. Study reminders use local notifications; the App does not use push notifications and does not collect a push token.

What we do not collect: we do not collect your location, we do not use advertising or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs, we do not collect an advertising identifier, and we do not access your contacts, photos, or files.

2. How we use your information

  • Operate the App (accounts, sign-in, saving your progress).
  • Generate AI Coach responses to your questions.
  • Process and validate purchases and unlock premium features.
  • Enforce free-tier usage limits and prevent abuse.
  • Diagnose crashes and improve reliability.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Data retention

We retain your account, profile, study, and Coach data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward. You can delete your account and associated data at any time (see Section 6).

5. Children's privacy

The App is intended for exam candidates and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us at tei_tech@outlook.com and we will delete it.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Delete your account: use the "Delete Account" option in the App (Profile screen), which permanently deletes your account and all associated data. You may also request deletion by emailing tei_tech@outlook.com.
  • Use anonymously: you can use the App without providing your email or name.
  • Voice input is optional: you can type instead of using the microphone.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted transport (HTTPS), server-side receipt validation, App Check attestation, and access-controlled database security rules. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

8. International transfers

Our service providers (including Google and Apple) may process data on servers located in the United States and other countries.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
